Chicken Soup

Description

A soul-warming meal best enjoyed in chilly weather or just when you want a nice, comforting something to dip your bread into!

(Source: https://www.tasteofhome.com/recipes/simple-chicken-soup/ visited 9/3/2022)

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (14-1/2 ounces each) reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
  • 1 package (16 ounces) frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 cups cubed cooked chicken breast
  • 2 cans (10-3/4 ounces each) reduced-fat reduced-sodium condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, bring broth and onion to a boil. Reduce heat.
  2. Add the vegetables
  3. cover and cook for 6-8 minutes or until crisp-tender.
  4. Stir in chicken and soup; heat through.
